Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, premium hosting ($240/year value), SSL certification, SEO optimization, and 12 months of maintenance and support. All projects include unlimited revisions during the design phase.

How long does it take to complete a website?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple websites can be done in as little as 7 days, while more complex e-commerce sites might take 6-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment plans with a 50% deposit to start and the remaining balance due upon completion. Monthly payment plans are available for larger projects. All major credit cards and PayPal are accepted.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, all websites include 12 months of maintenance, including security updates, backups, and technical support. After the first year, maintenance plans start at $99/month.

Can I update the website myself?

Absolutely! We build all websites on user-friendly content management systems and provide training on how to make basic updates. For more complex changes, our support team is always available to help.
